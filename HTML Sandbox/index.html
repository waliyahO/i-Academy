He who cannot be a good follower, cannot be a good leader

There is no leader without at least one follower—that’s obvious. Yet the modern leadership industry, is built on the proposition that leaders matter a great deal and followers hardly at all. Good leadership is the stuff of countless courses, workshops, books, and articles. Everyone wants to makes leaders tick—the charismatic ones, the retiring ones, and even the crooked ones.

Leadership

Our concept about leadership is that it is primarily an influencing role. This means that in a position of authority , an individual influences others and is influenced by the actions and the attitudes of followers.

The relationship between the two

Both positions are equally important, require responsibility, and interact with each other: the leader must help his followers grow, whereas the followers must contribute to the success of their leader
